# School Attendance Portal: 5-Minute Teacher Guide

Welcome to the new digital attendance portal for **VANIVILASAM L P SCHOOL**. This system replaces paper registers and handles all name call-outs and end-of-year calculations for you. 

Here is how to use it on your phone or computer.

---

## 1. How to Log In

1. Open the website address provided by the school on your phone.
2. Enter your school email address (e.g. `teacher@school.com`) and your password.
3. Tap **Sign In**.

*If you forget your password, ask the Principal to reset it for you in the admin dashboard.*

---

## 2. How to Mark Daily Attendance (Under 2 Minutes)

1. On the dashboard, tap the **Record Attendance** block.
2. Select your **Class** (e.g. `2A`) and confirm the **Date** is set to today.
3. Tap **Take Attendance**.
4. **All students are marked as PRESENT by default (checked).**
5. You only need to tap/uncheck the students who are **ABSENT** today.
6. When you mark a student absent, a box will appear. Enter a short reason if known (e.g. *sick*, *travel*, *marriage*).
7. Scroll to the bottom and tap the green **Save Attendance** button.

*A banner will show: "Attendance saved for 30 students" indicating a successful entry.*

---

## 3. How to View Class Reports & Export PDFs

If you need to print attendance sheets or count present days for report cards:

1. Open the side menu and select **Class Reports**.
2. Select your Class and choose a time range (e.g. **This Academic Year**).
3. You will see a table listing all your students, their present days, absent days, and exact percentage.
4. To save or print this:
   - Tap **Export Class PDF** to download a clean, print-friendly report card summary.
   - Tap **Bulk TC Format** if preparing records for students leaving mid-year.
